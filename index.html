FAQ

Short answers. No smoke.

Do I need to be a Meta Business Partner?

No. Rivet integrates with Meta Cloud API direct. Clients connect their WhatsApp business assets and pay WhatsApp fees directly.

Do you include WhatsApp message costs?

No. WhatsApp messaging fees are billed by WhatsApp. Rivet is billed separately as software.

We already have an API. Is Rivet still useful?

Yes. Rivet is API/webhook-first by default. The optional no-infra mode is just for pilots, SMEs, or teams whose APIs aren’t ready yet.

Can I roll back after publishing?

Yes. Rollback is part of Rivet’s deployment model so agencies can ship without fear.
