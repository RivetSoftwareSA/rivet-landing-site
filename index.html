How Rivet uses WhatsApp

Rivet is a software platform — not a messaging service. We give businesses the tools to build and run their own WhatsApp automation. Here's exactly how the WhatsApp Business Platform is used.

Transactional notifications
Order confirmations, appointment reminders, delivery updates, and payment receipts — sent to customers who have opted in.
Automated conversation flows
BotFlows that respond to inbound customer messages — bookings, lead qualification, FAQ responses, and support routing.
Template-based outbound messaging
Pre-approved Meta message templates for outbound notifications. All templates are submitted to and approved by Meta before use.
No unsolicited messaging
Rivet does not send bulk unsolicited messages. All usage complies with Meta's Platform Terms, WhatsApp Business Policy, and WhatsApp's Acceptable Use Policy.

Your clients' data is safe. Full stop.

Rivet focuses on isolation, auditability, and operational safety from day one.

Each client is fully separated
Every business on Rivet runs in its own isolated workspace — no data ever crosses between accounts.
Full history of every action
Every message sent, every change made, every publish logged — so you always know what happened and when.
Built-in compliance controls
Quiet hours, message rate limits, and safety checks built in — so you stay compliant with WhatsApp's rules automatically.
You control how long data is kept
Set your own data retention policies. Keep what you need, delete what you don't.

Common questions. Straight answers.

Do I need to be a Meta Business Partner?

No. Rivet connects directly to Meta's Cloud API. Your clients connect their own WhatsApp Business accounts and pay WhatsApp directly — no middleman.

Does Rivet include WhatsApp message costs?

No. WhatsApp charges you separately for messages. Rivet is just the software — you keep full control of your messaging costs.

We already have an API. Does Rivet still make sense?

Yes. Rivet is API and webhook-first. Connect it to your existing systems in minutes. The no-code mode is optional — for teams that aren't ready for API integration yet.

Can I undo a change after publishing?

Yes. Rivet has one-click rollback built in. Ship confidently — you can always go back to the last working version.

Do I need a developer to set up Rivet?

No. Rivet has a no-code mode that lets you build and publish flows without writing any code. If you have a developer, they can connect Rivet to your existing systems via API — but it's not required.

How long does it take to go live?

Most businesses go live within a day. Book a demo, pick a Pack for your industry, customize it for your business, and you're running.

What kinds of businesses use Rivet?

Logistics companies, medical clinics, real estate agencies, restaurants, and e-commerce stores. Any business using WhatsApp to communicate with customers can automate it with Rivet.

What happens if something goes wrong?

You get a full run log showing exactly what happened, step by step. And you can pause or roll back any flow instantly with one click.

Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?

Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.
